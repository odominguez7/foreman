# Launch posts

Three platform variants for the lightweight HW9 launch. Pick one or post all three; each is sized to its platform.

| File | Platform | Length | Tone | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [post-x.md](./post-x.md) | X (Twitter) | 6-tweet thread | Punchy, hook-led | Reach, founder visibility |
| [post-linkedin.md](./post-linkedin.md) | LinkedIn | ~280 words | Founder-narrative | Professional credibility, MIT network |
| [post-reddit.md](./post-reddit.md) | Reddit r/Machinists | Long-form | Peer-to-peer, honest about limitations | Direct shop owner reach, pilot leads |

## Recommended sequence (if posting to all three)

1. **Reddit first.** Honest, story-led, lowest marketing tone. If it lands, you have shop-owner comments to quote in the other two posts.
2. **LinkedIn next** (next morning). Use the LinkedIn post to credit MIT AI Studio and tag instructors. Drives the academic side.
3. **X last** (the same day as LinkedIn or one day later). The thread reaches developer / open-source folks who care about the agentic stack.

## Common rules across all three

- Lead with the founder story (11 years running the shop), not the product features.
- Be honest about limitations. r/Machinists especially rewards this.
- Ship the link to the live site (foremanjobs.lovable.app) and the repo (github.com/odominguez7/foreman).
- Reply to every comment for the first 60 minutes (X), 4 hours (LinkedIn), or 24 hours (Reddit). That is where the algorithmic / mod-trust signal comes from.
- Don't use the word "AI" in the body of any post. Buyers in this audience are AI-fatigued.
